About Me

I am an interdisciplinary researcher bridging political philosophy, linguistics, and AI. My current focus is on the Prediction–Plurality Paradox and how algorithmic infrastructures can sustain democratic plurality and political agency.

Research Interests

  • Algorithmic Natality & Democratic Theory
  • Political Agency in Computational Contexts
  • Philosophy of Technology and Democracy
  • Digital Public Spheres
