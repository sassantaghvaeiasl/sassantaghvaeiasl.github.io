About Me

I am an interdisciplinary researcher bridging political philosophy, linguistics, and AI. My current focus is on the Prediction–Plurality Paradox inspired by Hannah Arendt, exploring how algorithmic infrastructures can sustain democratic plurality and political agency.

Research Interests

  • Algorithmic Natality & Democratic Theory
  • Political Agency in Computational Contexts
  • Philosophy of Technology and Democracy
  • Digital Public Spheres

Selected Publications & Works

  • Review: Stiegler, The Age of Disruption, Iran Book News Agency (2025)
